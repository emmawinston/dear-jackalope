Soundbyte Template

view

The team who built the app, Soundbyte, on which I had worked during the 24-hour Accessibility Hackathon 2013 decided to place some information about the project online. I was tasked with building the page. With only a limited amount of time and no existing content, I elected to build a basic, one-page smooth-scrolling holding site, featuring simple animation to give the simple page a dynamic feel, even with placeholder images.

To save time, and ensure a fully responsive page, I used the Flat UI framework (a variant of Twitter Bootstrap) as the basis for the site.
