Brigitte D'Angelo

view

Brigitte D'Angelo, a designer and stylist, required a portfolio website in time for the launch of her new project ArtHaus. The pages had already been designed and needed to be developed for an appropriate CMS.

I built the pages within a Wordpress framework based on Automattic's Underscores. The focal point of Brigitte's site was the photos of her beautiful work - she was very selective about how she wanted them to be displayed, and wanted no more than three to appear on the page at any one time.

We selected the Everslider plugin for its customisability and beautiful UX, and implemented it on every page of the website. Everslider can also be operated by dragging on a touch device, or using a mousewheel.

I ensured the site was fully responsive, and implemented a mobile menu at small screen sizes to facilitate this.
