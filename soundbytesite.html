Sound Byte website

view

I rapidly developed a WordPress site over the course of a week for Sound Byte, a new startup aiming to create an Android app acting as a personally customisable hearing aid for occasional use. To increase the speed of the build and ensure a presentable site to be shown to the startup's funders even at an early stage of development, I built on top of Salient, a premium theme with an excellent modular CMS-style administration feature. Customising the theme (which has an extremely complex internal template structure and is really designed to be used out of the box, without customisation) to the extent required involved some highly specific and often creative modification of stylesheets, and represented a very different workflow from the from-scratch approach I normally take to Wordpress builds.
