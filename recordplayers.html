Record players

This was a poster created for a project run by a small group building cardboard record players with children (and sometimes adults!) at music festivals. I was sent several images of old shop signs and adverts and instructed to create a vintage-looking poster in two or three colours, with a hand-illustrated graphic of the record player itself.

I hope to do further work with the Cardboard Record Players workshop, creating a full website for their project, in the near future.
