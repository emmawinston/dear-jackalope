Jo Gordon

view

I acted as an assistant to lead developer Jason Hart for knitwear designer Jo Gordon's Shopify-based website, debugging code and making stylistic changes to template files. Jo requested a WordPress-based blog built on a template identical to her existing Shopify website. Over the course of half a day I also delivered a very simple Wordpress theme for Jo's blog, identical in appearance to her Shopify theme - minimal, clean, and perfect for showcasing Jo's vibrant knitwear.
