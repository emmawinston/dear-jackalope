About

What I do

I'm Emma Winston, and I'm a freelance web designer who loves creating beautiful and usable interfaces. Whether working alone or as a member of your team, I aim to provide a flexible, friendly and stress-free service to build you something lovely and bring your web presence to life.

I work in accordance with the W3C's guidelines for web standards, and I'm also passionate about accessibility - I aim for all my websites to welcome users with diverse needs and abilities, and will consider this a key component of anything I build for you.

Education

I've tinkered with code for almost as long as I can remember, but gained my formal training with Prisca Schmarsow on Tower Hamlets College's Design For The Web diploma.
